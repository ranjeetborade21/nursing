Nursing is one of the best career option available to the any stream students after HSC.

Our COURSES

ANM [Auxiliary Nursing And Midwifery]

Auxiliary Nursing And Midwifery or ANM for short, is a 2 year undergraduate diploma course in nursing. The minimum eligibility criteria to get admitted into Auxiliary Nursing And Midwifery course is to be a minimum 10+2 level pass out with a minimum aggregate score of at least 40% from any stream. ANM students are educated on maternity care and providing assistance to new mothers during pregnancy and post-delivery.

GNM [General Nursing And Midwifery]

It is a 3½ years diploma in paramedics. Nursing and Midwifery focuses on the provision of care to individuals, families, and communities. The course can be pursued after successful completion of the 10+2 level of education in the Preferable Science Stream or Art Stream or Commerce Stream from a recognized educational board with a minimum aggregate score of 40%.
